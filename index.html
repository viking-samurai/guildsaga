Claim your Guild Hero

Play with your Hero in the upcoming Solana play-to-earn fantasy RPG: Guild Saga!

All Heros come with:

  • Detailed generative pixel art NFTs that can be imported into the game on release
  • Over 700 available traits, with all equipment (except jewelry, belt, and gloves) being displayed in both on-character and inventory form

Game-ready NFTs randomly generated from over 700 possible traits!

Guild Hero NFTs have 18 attributes, with the inventory view background colour indicating their quality (common, uncommon, rare, etc). The border of each NFT indicates its overall rarity level, determined by summing the quality of each item.

Get your Hero on:

Magic Eden, Solanart, Fractal, or NFT marketplaces.

Enjoy all of the in-game features, such as:

Fully-Customizable Characters

Select gender, skin tone, eye colour, and hairstyle in Guild Saga’s flexible character creator. Changes to clothing, head equipment, back equipment, boots, and weapons are visible on the in-game character—all thanks to tens of thousands of hand-drawn sprites by our world-class Lead Pixel Artist.

Guilds

Players can band together in Guilds and complete guild-specific quests to earn tokens for themselves and their guild’s treasury. Guild wars provide players with the opportunity to pillage the coffers of rival guilds through PVP battles.

Diverse Build Potential

Players can mix and match skills from 10 different schools to create unique builds and execute powerful skill combinations.

Plus, don't forget...

Combat

Guild Saga features turn-based, tactics-style combat heavily inspired by classics like Final Fantasy Tactics and Tactics Ogre as well as modern CRPGs like Divinity: Original Sin and Pillars of Eternity.

Open World Concept

Guild Saga takes full advantage of the opportunities that blockchain integration provides to incentivize exploration. Plenty of (tokenized) treasure, quests, and easter eggs lay concealed across Guild Saga’s sprawling open world, ready for discovery by only the most inquisitive adventurers.

Play-to-Earn Mechanics

Rich Storytelling

The story of Guild Saga is thoughtfully written with branching dialogue and meaningful decision-making. A lowly member of the Heroes Guild, the player character is surprised when they are selected as a field agent to journey to the Andra, a young continent where mysterious and destructive occurrences are transpiring beyond the bounds of civilization.

Game Developments

Extended Gameplay Video

An extended game play video of the opening part of the Tales of Respite demo, show casing character creation, NFT import, dialogue, battles, quests, cinematicevents, exploration, looting, and more.

Tales of Respite Demo

A feature-complete 3hour+ demo showcasing the opening Act of the game, Tales of Respite. In addition to providing users with an early look at the game, Tales of Respite will serve as the game's beta: a testing environment aimed at effective skill balancing, detectingbugs, studying earning patterns, and soliciting feedback on game features and mechanics.

A playable build of the Extended Gameplay Video provided to influencers to show case the game to their audience and provide feedback to the team.

Public Beta

Dev-net GGLD, GGEM, and item NFT earnings are added to the Tales of Respite demo, providing a testing ground for on-chain earnings.

Mainnet Launch

GuildSaga, including Part 1 (TalesofRespite) and Part 2 of the story mode, plusArenaMode, is launched on Solana main net. Players will be able to use their Hero NFTs and earn GGLD, GGEM, and item NFTs.

Frontier Mode

Join other players in hunting monsters beyond the Frontier, build up your land, and team up with your guild in the PVP wilderness in this MMO mode.

PVP mode Alpha

In the PVP mode, players compete against each other in four-on-four battles (with one player controlling all four characters on each side) on battlefields similar to those in the single-player arena mode.

PVE Arena Mode Alpha

A public alpha where player’s can enjoy the first iteration of GuildSaga’s gauntlet-style arena mode, where they will compete against progressively more difficult AI opponents. This mode can be played in parallel to the story mode and provides an infinite endgame with daily earning limits.

World Developments

Heroes' Guild (NFT Staking)

At the Heroes Guild, holders of a Hero NFT can send their Hero on quests. While on a quest, Heroes will earn $GGLD, $GGEM, and loot(item NFTs).

Blacksmith (NFTCrafting)

At the blacksmith, users can combine loot/item NFTs. The item NFTsused to craft are burned and the user receives a new item in their place. For example, 3 gold ore may combine to a gold bar, and 3 gold bars may combine to make a new piece of equipment.

Town Square (Item NFT Marketplace)

The Town Square is New acre's marketplace. Here, users can purchase and sell loot and mercenary NFTs on GuildSaga's $GGLD token marketplace.

Catacombs (Mercenary NFT Summoning)

At the Catacombs, players can summon Mercenary NFT susing $GGLD and $GGEM. A Hero NFT is also required to act as the summoner, but is not consumed. Summoning will take a specified amount of time to complete

Royal Bank ($GGLDStaking)

At the Royal Bank, users can open a vault and deposit $GGLD. Upon deposit, the $GGLD is locked up and earns the user passive $GGLD over time in an amount proportional to the amount staked.

Player’s Keep (Base-building and Quests)

At the player's Keep, MercenaryNFTs can be sent on quests or stationed to guard the Keep. Stationing a Mercenary at a keep will provide a bonus to $GGLD or resource accrual as well as affect Keep defense success. Following there lease of the in-game Keep system, the World Mode Keep location will integrate with the Player wallet's in-game keep, providing an alternative method to monitor and manage your base.
